  Combine 30 ml acetone and 50 ml hydrogen peroxide into glass container, mix thoroughly.
  Take coffee can, fill halfway with water and salt.
  Place container into coffee can, cover can, and put in freezer; wait til -5 degrees C.
  Add 2.5 ml of sulfuric acid drop by drop; test temperature after each drop making sure it doesn't go past 10 degrees C.
  Stir and then place in fridge; let sit overnight.
  Take plastic container, cover with paper towel, pour solution from glass container over paper towel.
  Pour 3 spoonfuls of ice-cold water through the towel to remove acid; set paper towel to dry.
  Pour remaining crystals into plastic container; place magnesium ribbon; cover with lid.
  Light magnesium ribbon and walk away whistling a happy tune.
