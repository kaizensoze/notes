to figure out scale:
    just remember the step intervals
    tonic supertonic mediant subdominant dominant submediant leading/subtonic

to figure out key conversions:
    a flat is -1, a sharp is +1
    C -> C#: +7
    C -> Cb: -7
    C(major) -> c(minor): -3
